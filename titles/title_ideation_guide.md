# Title Ideation Guide & Psychology

Based on the analysis of top-performing titles in the AI & Automation space.

## The Psychology of the Click

### 1. The "Insider Secret" Frame
*   **Concept:** People fear missing out on a shortcut that others are using.
*   **Keywords:** "Cheat Code", "Unfair Advantage", "Hack", "Secret", "Glitch", "Nobody Uses".
*   **Examples:**
    *   "n8n's New MCP Feature Is A **CHEAT CODE**"
    *   "The EMOJI Prompting **Cheat Code Nobody Uses**"
    *   "This ONE Trick Generates Prompts for **EVERY AI Model**"

### 2. The "Pattern Interrupt" / Contrarian
*   **Concept:** Go against the common narrative or warn the user they are doing something wrong. This stops the scroll.
*   **Keywords:** "Stop", "Dying", "Don't", "Wrecked", "Trap", "Lie", "Harsh Truth".
*   **Examples:**
    *   "**Stop Learning** n8n in 2025... Learn THIS Instead"
    *   "n8n is **DYING**"
    *   "Why You Probably **Shouldn't** Fine-Tune Your AI Model"
    *   "The AI **Trap Everyone Falls For**"

### 3. Extreme Efficiency (Speed & Ease)
*   **Concept:** The promise of high results for low effort/time.
*   **Keywords:** "Instantly", "In Minutes", "Seconds", "Easy", "No Code", "10X".
*   **Examples:**
    *   "How to **INSTANTLY** Generate Claude Skills at **SCALE**"
    *   "Build Your Own Claude SKILLS in **under 15 Minutes!**"
    *   "Gemini's File Search API Makes RAG **Easy (and CHEAP!)**"

### 4. High-Stakes Curiosity / FOMO
*   **Concept:** A title that poses a question or scenario the viewer *needs* to resolve.
*   **Keywords:** "Terrifying", "Absurd", "Golden Age", "Changes Everything".
*   **Examples:**
    *   "I Made an AI Version of Myself and **It's Terrifying**"
    *   "This Is What Happens When You Combine Claude and Sora 2" (Implied explosive result)

### 5. The "Service" / Validation
*   **Concept:** You did the hard work so the viewer doesn't have to. Or you are validating a new tool.
*   **Keywords:** "I Tested", "So You Don't Have To", "Battle Tested", "Ranking".
*   **Examples:**
    *   "I Tested ChatGPT's NEW Thinking Modes **So You Don't Have To**"
    *   "I **Battle Tested** n8n's NEW NATIVE AI Builder"

## Title Structure Formulas

| Formula Name | Structure | Example |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **The "How-To" Benefit** | How to [Action] [High Benefit] with [Tool] | "How to **CHEAT Time** and Get **10X More Done** With AI" |
| **The Negative Warning** | Stop [Common Action]... Do [This] Instead | "**Stop Building** n8n AI Agents... Use This Claude MCP" |
| **The "One Thing"** | This ONE [Trick/Tool] [Big Result] | "This **ONE Vibe Coding Trick** Will Help You Build Faster" |
| **The Identity/Outcome** | Become a [Desirable Identity] with [Tool] | "Claude Code Masterclass: **Become a ONE-Person Company**" |
| **The Prediction** | [Year] Will Be The [Adjective] Age of [Topic] | "**2026** Will Be The **Golden Age** of AI Consulting" |
| **The Insider Reveal** | The [Tool/Feature] [Authority] Don't Want You To Know | "The **Secrets** of Claude Code From the **Engineers Who Built It**" |

## Mark Kashef's "Sweet Spot"
Based on your greatest hits, your audience responds best to:
1.  **Personal Experiments with "Scary" or "High" Outcomes:** ("I Made an AI Version of Myself", "Terrifying")
2.  **Forward-Looking Authority:** ("2026 Golden Age", "How I'd Learn AI for 2026")
3.  **Specific Technical "Cheats":** ("Cheat Code", "Elite", "10X Productivity")
4.  **Comparison/Killer Titles:** ("n8n Just Killed Google Sheets", "Replace Google")

## Optimization Tips
*   **Character Count:** Aim for **50-60 characters** for optimal visibility on all devices.
*   **Front-Loading:** Put the most important words (keywords, emotional triggers) at the **beginning** of the title.
*   **Capitalization:** Use ALL CAPS for 1-2 emphasis words (e.g., "STOP", "CHEAT CODE"). Don't overdo it.
*   **Brackets:** Use brackets `[ ]` or parentheses `( )` to add context or a secondary hook at the end (e.g., "(No Code)", "[Complete Course]").
