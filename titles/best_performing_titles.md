## Common Themes & Patterns
1.  **"Cheat Code" / "Unfair Advantage" / "Hack":** Implying a shortcut to success.
2.  **"Absurd" / "Nuts":** High energy adjectives to describe new capabilities.
3.  **"Stop Doing X" / "Dying":** Negative framing to grab attention (Pattern Interrupt).
4.  **"I Tested X So You Don't Have To":** Service-based value proposition.
5.  **"How I..." (Personal Stories):** Vulnerability and personal experience ("after wasting 18 months").
6.  **"Complete Guide" / "Masterclass":** Promising comprehensive knowledge.
7.  **Time-Bound Promises:** "In Minutes", "Instantly", "For 2026".
